My Radios

You'll find a list of internet radio stations, over 100 in all.

You can scroll, filter or search for your station. You can make changes in the file "moodies.csv"

Squeezers

Click the Squeezelite button to open a modal for your favorite Squeezebox players.

With a Spotify account, this is a must see.
